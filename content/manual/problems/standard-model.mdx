A central structural result concerns the confining multiplicity $N$ the

This is the Standard Model hypercharge assignment derived, not assumed.

**GI$^{++}$ (record-coherent abelian coupling):** the Higgs scalar carries
nonzero charge under the rank-one abelian channel. This excludes the
anomaly-free but record-decoupled alternatives where $h_u = h_d = 0$.

With charge classification complete, the **Witten global $\mathrm{SU}(2)$
